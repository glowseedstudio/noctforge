Privacy & Security

Encrypted. Hardware-backed. Offline.

No INTERNET permission in the manifest. No analytics SDK. No crash reporting phoning home. No account required for anything.

🔐

XChaCha20-Poly1305

AEAD encryption for notes, vault, and authenticator secrets. Random nonce per encryption. MAC verification on every read.

🔑

Android Keystore

Master keys are hardware-backed. Biometric binding available. Keys never leave the secure element.

📵

No Internet Permission

The INTERNET permission is explicitly removed from the Android manifest. The app cannot phone home. Provably offline.

🛡️

FLAG_SECURE

Every sensitive screen blocks screenshots and screen recording at the OS level. No screenshot app or screen recorder can capture your private data.

🗝️

300K PBKDF2 Iterations

Encrypted backups use PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 with 300,000 iterations. Passphrase brute-force is computationally infeasible.

🫥

Decoy Vault

NoctForge Notes supports a full decoy vault with a separate PIN. Under duress, open the decoy — your real data stays hidden.
