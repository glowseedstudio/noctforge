Privacy

No internet. No excuses.

NoctForge holds no internet permission. There is no server to breach, no account to compromise, no telemetry draining your battery. Just software that works and stays quiet.

🔐

XChaCha20-Poly1305

AEAD encryption for notes, vault, and authenticator secrets. Random nonce per encryption. MAC verification on every read.

🔑

Android Keystore

Master keys are hardware-backed on supported devices. Biometric binding available. Keys never leave the secure element.

📵

No Internet Permission

The INTERNET permission is explicitly removed from the Android manifest. The app cannot phone home. Provably offline.

🛡️

FLAG_SECURE

Every sensitive screen blocks screenshots and screen recording at the OS level. No screenshot app or screen recorder can capture your private data.

🗝️

600K PBKDF2 Iterations

Encrypted backups use PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 with 600,000 iterations. Passphrase brute-force is computationally infeasible.

🫥

Decoy Vault

NoctForge Notes supports a full decoy vault with a separate PIN. Under duress, open the decoy — your real data stays hidden.

💾

Encrypted Local Backups

Your notes and 2FA keys can be manually exported as an encrypted backup file — stored wherever you choose, on your device. If your phone breaks or you switch devices, your data comes with you. Nothing is ever locked to a cloud account.

📦

Closed Source — Honest About It

NoctForge is proprietary, closed-source software. We're upfront about that. What we can tell you: no internet permission in the manifest (provably offline), all sensitive features documented in our permissions breakdown, and every privacy claim here is verifiable by behaviour — not just words.

🔬

On-Device OCR — No Google Play Services Required

PDF OCR uses a fully bundled on-device text recognition model — no Google Play Services, no network call, no account. Document scanning runs on OpenCV, also entirely bundled inside the app. Neither component can communicate with the outside world. Your documents stay on your device.

🛠️

Custom ROM Friendly

NoctForge requires Android 13+ and has zero Google Play Services dependencies. It runs on de-Googled devices including GrapheneOS, LineageOS, and CalyxOS — no GMS, no Play Store account required for functionality once installed.

Note for Custom ROM users: NoctForge is launching first on Google Play. Because the app has no INTERNET permission, it cannot use standard online license-checking. We are actively setting up a direct-purchase option (Gumroad/Ko-fi) to offer a completely DRM-free APK for users who do not use the Play Store. Stay tuned.

Transparency

Why we ask for what we ask.

NoctForge requests only what specific features need to function. Every permission can be revoked at any time in Android Settings — the app will simply disable the feature that needs it, nothing else changes.

📷
Camera
Used by the Flashlight, QR Scanner, and camera tools. Only activated when you open one of these features. Never runs in the background.
🎙️
Microphone
Used by the Instrument Tuner for pitch detection and Voice Memos for recording. Only active while you're in those tools. No audio is stored or transmitted.
🔊
Modify Audio Settings
Lets the Metronome and music player manage audio focus and routing correctly — so playback pauses when a call comes in and resumes after.
👆
Biometrics / Fingerprint
Used to lock and unlock sensitive features like the Authenticator and encrypted notes. Biometric data never leaves your device — it's handled entirely by Android.
🧭
Precise Location
Used by the Compass and Heading HUD for GPS-based bearing accuracy. Only read when those tools are open. No location data is stored, logged, or sent anywhere.
🔵
Bluetooth Connect
Used by Device Info to display the names of connected Bluetooth devices. Read-only — NoctForge does not initiate or manage Bluetooth connections.
💊
Display Over Other Apps
Required for NoctPill, the floating overlay. Without this permission NoctPill cannot appear above other apps. You grant this manually in system settings — Android requires explicit approval.
🔕
Notification Access
Used by NoctPill to show incoming notifications and currently playing media from other apps in the overlay. You control which apps are included — any app can be toggled off individually in NoctPill settings. Granted manually via Settings → Apps → Special app access → Notification access. Nothing is stored, logged, or transmitted.
🎵
Read Media Audio
Lets the music player read your local music library. Only accesses audio files. No other files are read, and nothing leaves your device.
🌙
Wake Lock
Keeps the screen on during the Bedside Clock and prevents the processor from sleeping during music playback. Released immediately when you leave those features.
🔔
Notifications
Used for timer alerts, reminders, and the music player notification. Only requested when you enable these features. You can revoke it and the rest of the app works fine.
⏱️
Exact Alarms
Makes timers and reminders fire precisely on time rather than in a window. Android 14+ may ask you to approve this separately in system settings.
⚙️
Boot Completed / Foreground Services
Boot Completed restores scheduled reminders after a restart. Foreground Services keep music playing and NoctPill running when you switch apps — Android requires this for anything persistent.

What we explicitly do not request: Internet access, external storage, network state, Wi-Fi state, or advertising ID. The INTERNET permission is removed from the app manifest entirely — NoctForge is provably unable to send data anywhere.
